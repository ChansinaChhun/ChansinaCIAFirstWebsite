CIA FIRST INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL

CIA FIRST International School’s curriculum is unique and innovative. Piece by piece, the academic team has created a curricular program
that focuses on global competencies and 21st-century skills. By using the Understanding by Design Framework (UbD), and integrating the Habits of Mind (HoMs),
CIA FIRST student develops long-term and transferable understandings, which they can apply to real situations
and solve real-world problems (Schoolwide Learner Outcomes).

American AERO Standards

CIA FIRST adheres to a rigorous and balanced American curriculum in Cambodia that is directly aligned with the AERO and the US Common Core standards, issued by the United States Department of Education. CIA FIRST has been awarded accreditation by the Western Association of Schools and Colleges (WASC) for grades PK – 12. The Ministry of Education also gave authorization for our international high school diploma to be officially recognized as equivalent to the National Grade 12 Khmer curriculum diploma.
